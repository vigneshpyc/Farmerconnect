Tomato

Tomato is a popular fruit commonly used as a vegetable in cooking. It comes in various sizes, shapes, and colors, with red being the most common. Tomatoes are rich in vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants, known for their health benefits. They're versatile ingredients, used in salads, sauces, soups, and sandwiches, adding flavor and nutrition to a wide range of dishes.

Onion

Onion is a bulb vegetable widely used in cooking for its pungent flavor and aroma. It comes in various colors, including white, yellow, and red. Onions are rich in antioxidants and nutrients like vitamin C and fiber. They can be eaten raw, cooked, or caramelized, adding depth of flavor to dishes such as soups, stews, salads, and stir-fries. Additionally, onions are a staple ingredient in many cuisines worldwide.

Potato

Potato is a starchy tuberous crop known for its versatility in cooking and nutritional value. With a variety of types ranging from russet to red to sweet, potatoes offer an array of flavors and textures.` Potatoes can be boiled, baked, mashed, fried, or roasted, making them a fundamental ingredient in dishes like fries, chips, soups, and casseroles, offering both comfort and sustenance.

Cabbage

Cabbage is a leafy green or purple vegetable belonging to the Brassica family, prized for its crunchy texture and mild flavor. Rich in vitamins K, C, and fiber, cabbage offers numerous health benefits. It's versatile in cooking, being used raw in salads, fermented as sauerkraut, or cooked in soups, stir-fries, and stews. With its ability to be easily incorporated into various dishes.
